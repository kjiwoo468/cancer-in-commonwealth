Good to know

How this site works

Built for going at your own speed

Each module is a sequence of short sections. Use the Next and Back buttons, or click any numbered marker on the trail at the top of a module to jump straight to a section. There's no time limit — re-read, skip ahead, or come back tomorrow.

Your progress stays on this device

This site remembers which sections you've viewed and how you scored on each module's quiz — right in your browser, on this device. It isn't sent anywhere or shared with anyone else.

Activities and quizzes give instant feedback

Throughout each module you'll find short activities, reflection questions, and a knowledge-check quiz. Click an answer to see right away whether you got it and why — there's no grade, just a chance to check your understanding.

Certificates & starting over

Finish a module's post-test to unlock a printable certificate of completion for that module. Finish all three to unlock a full-course certificate.

About the curriculum

Where this content comes from

The lessons on this site are adapted from Cancer in the Commonwealth, a cancer education curriculum developed by the University of Kentucky Markey Cancer Center specifically for students in Appalachian Kentucky. The original curriculum covers cancer biology, regional health disparities, modifiable risk factors, and how cancer is diagnosed and treated — with an emphasis on the issues that matter most to rural Kentucky communities.
